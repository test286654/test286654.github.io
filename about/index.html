ABOUT US


We began as an MSP and MSSP, covering all areas of information technology and cybersecurity. We implemented highly complex IT systems and cutting-edge, next generation malware protection for the cloud, endpoints, application-layers and networks.

Today, we have used the knowledge we’ve gathered from supporting various clients in industries such as Healthcare (John Hopkins), Real Estate (Win Morrison Realty), Government (New York State, City of Philadelphia), Hospitality (Buttermilk Falls Inn & Spa), Construction (iBUILT Group), etc.. – and applied our knowledge to solve difficult problems in the Software-as-a-Service space.

You use Microsoft Office 365, Google Workspace (formerly GSuite) among others. They have geo-redundant storage facilities. They are protecting my data, or so you think.

The reality is that the big cloud app publishers are focused on making sure the software they sell works without interruptions or problems. Operational infrastructure is their primary concern. They want to be sure that when a server fails, there is an instantaneous failover available. Their customers will not miss a beat. They are not concerned with storing or protecting your data. It’s explicitly stated in the Terms of Service that users are responsible for their data. Our mission is to fill the gap of data protection in areas that aren't frequently covered.
